PRAVAAH

PRAVAAH is the annual Cultural, Techno-Entrepreneurship Fest of IIT Bhubaneswar, celebrating innovation, creativity, and vibrant campus spirit. Entering its 2nd grand edition, PRAVAAH brings together the energy of culture, the brilliance of technology, and the vision of entrepreneurship into one unforgettable experience. It stands as a platform where talent meets opportunity, ideas meet innovation, and students from across the nation come together to create, compete, and celebrate. "Chronicles of Time" is an epic journey across the eras, inviting participants to celebrate the Past through events that revive retro cultural aesthetics and timeless artistic traditions; to ground themselves in the Present with high-stakes quantitative entrepreneurship challenges focused on cutting-edge finance and real-time innovation; and finally, to propel themselves into the Future by tackling the boundless, conceptual frontiers of space technology and extraterrestrial exploration.
